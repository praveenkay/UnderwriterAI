ZURICH INSURANCE UNDERWRITING GUIDELINES

Policy Amendment Rules:
1. Coverage increases up to 25% can be auto-approved for low-risk clients
2. Premium adjustments require senior review for amounts over £10,000
3. Renewal discounts of 5-10% apply to clients with no claims for 3+ years
4. High-risk business types require escalation to underwriting team

Risk Assessment Criteria:
- Restaurant businesses: Medium risk due to fire/liability exposure
- Technology companies: Low risk for general liability
- Manufacturing: High risk requiring detailed assessment

Automatic Approval Conditions:
- Policy renewals with no changes: Auto-approve
- Coverage decreases: Auto-approve with retention team notification
- Premium increases under 15%: Auto-approve for existing clients
