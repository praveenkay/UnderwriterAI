Zurich SME Underwriting Guidelines - Sample Extract
===================================================

RENEWAL DISCOUNTS
- 5% discount available for clients with no claims for 3+ years
- 7% discount for clients with 5+ years clean history
- 10% maximum discount for exceptional clients (10+ years, no claims)

COVERAGE LIMITS
- Standard SME maximum coverage: £1,000,000
- Restaurant/hospitality maximum: £750,000 (higher with risk assessment)
- Tech startups: £500,000 initial, £1,000,000 after 2 years
- Manufacturing: £2,000,000 maximum

RISK ASSESSMENT TRIGGERS
- Previous fire claims for restaurants require manual review for coverage increases
- Claims exceeding £50,000 trigger enhanced underwriting
- High-risk industries (construction, chemicals) require senior approval
- Coverage increases above 50% of current policy need review

BUSINESS INTERRUPTION
- Standard coverage up to £200,000 for most SMEs
- Manufacturing and retail: up to £500,000
- Restaurants and hospitality: up to £150,000 standard

AUTOMATIC APPROVALS
- Renewals with no changes: automatic
- Discounts up to 5% for qualifying clients: automatic
- Coverage increases up to 25% for low-risk clients: automatic
- Policy amendments (address, contact details): automatic

ESCALATION REQUIREMENTS
- Coverage increases above maximum limits
- Clients with multiple recent claims
- High-risk industry applications
- Discounts exceeding standard guidelines
- Claims history indicates pattern of negligence
